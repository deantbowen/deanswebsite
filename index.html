About me

I have most recently served as a Cyber Security Analyst II, where I’ve been responsible for overseeing comprehensive threat analysis, managing detection and response systems (MDR), and strengthening security operations for the past 1 year and 6 months. In this role, I have specialized in key areas such as vulnerability management, web application security, and email security, ensuring strong defenses against evolving cyber threats. I’ve also played a key role in real-time monitoring and proactive threat hunting, consistently adapting to emerging security challenges.

I am inspired to be a Security Analyst because of my passion for problem-solving and the constantly evolving nature of cybersecurity. The thrill of identifying and neutralizing threats, coupled with the opportunity to protect organizations from cyberattacks, drives my commitment to the field. I find it deeply rewarding to stay ahead of cybercriminals, using advanced tools and techniques to secure sensitive data and critical systems. The dynamic nature of cybersecurity challenges me to continuously learn, adapt, and improve, ensuring that I can make a meaningful impact in safeguarding the digital world.
